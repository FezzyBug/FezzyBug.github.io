Project Overview

Scene Seen is a small VR media recording service startup specializing in immersive experiences for events. We enable clients to "relive" moments in 3D VR, from concerts and sports games to personal milestones.

  • Purpose: Provide VR recording services for events and personal experiences.
  • Intended Users: Event organizers, businesses, and individuals looking for high-quality VR recordings.
  • Content: Details of services, client testimonials, sample recordings, and booking information.
