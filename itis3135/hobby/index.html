How to Choose Headphones

Choosing the right headphones is a deeply personal journey. Here are my top considerations:

Criteria Description
Sound Quality Each headphone brings out a different aspect of the music. Finding a balanced yet detailed sound is crucial.
Comfort For long sessions, comfort is key. The ZMF Aeolus is a favorite for its warmth and softness.
Personalization Customization can add a unique touch, like with my modded TH-900.
Build Quality High-quality materials enhance durability. Metal or reinforced plastic frames usually hold up better over time.
Portability Depending on where you plan to use your headphones, consider weight and foldability for easier transport.
Noise Isolation If you’re often in noisy environments, good passive or active noise isolation can greatly improve the listening experience.
Price-to-Performance Ratio It's essential to find the best quality within your budget. Some headphones offer excellent performance without a steep price tag.
Type of Sound Signature Consider if you prefer a warmer sound, a bright sound, or something neutral. This can dramatically change your listening experience.

The Aeolus brings warmth and organic sound that remains unmatched as one of my favorite headphones (following the criteria above).
